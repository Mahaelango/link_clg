OVERVIEW

To contribute to society by developing green materials, bio-inspired materials and healthcare-associated products and technologies, demand for B.Tech. - Biotechnology in industries is increasing every year. Application of biological insight is also becoming popular in healthcare and industries such as materials, chemicals, food and fuels.

With a strong foundation in chemical and biological sciences and bioprocess engineering, MUJ biotechnology engineers will acquire excellent skills to tackle the complex problems society is facing. The engineering degree program B.Tech. - Biotechnology prepares students for careers in healthcare, industry, and research in emerging areas including chemical, biologicals, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, brewery, biomaterials, biomedical engineering, water treatment, biofuels, and enzyme processing industries etc. It is also an ideal program for students planning to pursue higher education in India and abroad.
